Our Core Business

Based on the German market, we provide one-stop logistics services covering the whole supply chain, and our business scope is in line with the registered business content of the enterprise

Freight Transportation

Professional road freight transportation services in Europe, providing door-to-door transportation solutions for general goods and special goods, with complete transportation qualifications and compliance with EU logistics regulations.

Warehousing Services

Provide standard and customized warehousing services in Neuss and other core areas of Germany, including goods storage, sorting, packaging and transshipment, to meet the warehousing needs of cross-border goods.

Delivery Service

Provide efficient terminal delivery services covering the whole of Germany and even Europe, coordinate with the upstream transportation links, and ensure the timely and safe arrival of goods.

Supply Chain Management

Customize integrated supply chain solutions for customers, including logistics planning, information tracking, cost control and risk management, to help customers optimize the whole logistics link.
